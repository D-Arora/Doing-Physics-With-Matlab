INTRODUCTION

 

The system we will simulate is a pendulum where a mass m is attached to a rigid massless rod so that the centre of mass is a distance L from a frictionless pivot. There is viscous damping of the motion and an applied sinusoidal driving force acting on the pendulum. The system is constrained to move in a plane. The net force acting on the system is the resultant force due to the gravitational force ; a viscous drag force  and an externally applied sinusoidal driving force .  The motion is described in terms of the angular displacement , the angular velocity , and the angular acceleration .
